I’m an engineer. I enjoy solving interesting problems and building digital products. I'm also a sucker for the thrill of early stage startups.

I learnt the most profound lessons in life from other humans, and this reflects in my philosophy on software and product design: Always put the people first.

Most days I write code, learn new things, and write about my experiences doing the first two. I'm passionate about building tools that enable people to be productive and do work that is fullfilling.

LiveDocs, my current project, is a programmable text editor for futuristic companies. With LiveDocs, you no longer have to manually scourge hundreds of tools (like analytics, CRMs, etc) for data and then plug it into your reports and other documents. LiveDocs helps you create self-updating documents with live data from your existing tools.
